What is Konnecteum

Konnecteum is the next generation AI-blockchain enabled freelancing platform that helps to build trust among freelancers and employers. Every member onboarded onto the platform is verified through a background check. At Konnecteum we aim to revolutionize the Freelancing and Job Markets. Konnecteum plans to lead the way for a smarter future, connecting the right talent with the right jobs in a trustworthy and secure way. It offers transparent and fair platform with lowest possible fees. Our background verification feature encourages small as well as large corporates along with individual employers to find talent for niche jobs and critical work. Language translation feature plans to overcome the communication issues across the globe. Konnecteum plans to make the VR meetings a reality.

Freelancer

Traditionally, startups and smaller businesses hire freelancers for short-term ad hoc projects that the owners and their staff don't have time to do themselves. This trend has begun to change recently,as companies are integrating freelancers into their core business strategies

Employer

An employer is an organization, institution, government entity, agency, company, professional services firm, nonprofit association, small business, store, or individual who employs or puts to work, a person who is called an employee or a staff member.

Partner

A business partnership is a specific kind of legal relationship formed by the agreement between two or more individuals to carry on a business as co-owners. A partnership is a business with multiple owners, each of whom has invested in the business.
